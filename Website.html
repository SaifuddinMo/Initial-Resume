I came up with the idea of developing devices that worked on hydraulic pressure. I made use of syringes filled with water as a source of energy. Just to sum it up it worked on the famous Pascal's Law. It wasn’t quite surprising that I would have bagged the first prize because I did. My devices which included the hydraulic brake, hydraulic tree shifter and the hydraulic lift were really captivating for the judges. I was able to achieve the First prize in the entire of Saudi Arabia.
